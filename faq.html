Contact Us
Do I need to book an appointment?

Walk-ins are welcome when a stylist is available. For colour, perming, rebonding, treatment, or weekend visits, contact us first so we can reserve enough time.

Where is Top Design Salon located?

We are at Lor 7 Toa Payoh, #01-258, Singapore 310018, near Kim Keat Palm Market & Food Centre.

What are your opening hours?

Monday to Saturday: 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Sunday: 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

How much do services cost?

Haircuts start from $28, wash and blowdry from $18, treatments from $45, colouring from $80, and perming or rebonding from $120. Final pricing depends on hair length, condition, and the service plan. We will confirm the expected cost before starting.

How do membership cards work?

Membership cards let you top up salon credit and receive bonus value. The credit can be used across salon services, which makes it useful for regular trims, treatments, colour maintenance, and other upkeep.

Can I consult before deciding on colour or perming?

Yes. We recommend a consultation for chemical services so your stylist can assess your hair condition, hair history, maintenance expectations, and whether the result you want is realistic before starting.

How can I contact the salon?

You can call the store at 69098342 or message the team on WhatsApp from the Contact page.
