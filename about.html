The Heart of the Craft

A Decade of Stories, One Chair at a Time

Top Design Salon isn't just a business; it's a craft house led by Kerry and Xiao Lyu. For over ten years, we have been a part of the Toa Payoh routine, earning our place through steady quality and honest advice.

We are the salon that says 'No.' If a treatment will compromise your hair's integrity, we won't do it. We prioritize the Tuesday morning—the moment two weeks after your visit when you can still style your hair with ease. That is the true test of a great cut.
